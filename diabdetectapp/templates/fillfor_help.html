Precautions to Take

As You Know Not Only Medicine can Cure the Diabeties

Here are 12 ways to lower blood sugar naturally, many of which also have benefits on overall health.

Limit Carbs
Carbohydrates are broken down and absorbed as sugar in the body. Sweets, refined grains, juices, and sodas raise blood sugar quickly. Replacing simple carbs with complex carbs such as whole grains and vegetables helps control blood sugar levels.

Drink Water
Staying hydrated helps prevent blood sugar concentration. Avoid sugary beverages and stick to water.

Add Fiber to Your Diet
Fiber does not increase blood sugar and helps improve digestion, cholesterol, and fullness.

Add Raw or Cooked Garlic
Garlic may help improve insulin sensitivity and overall heart health.

Try Probiotics
Found in yogurt and fermented foods, probiotics can help reduce fasting blood sugar and improve insulin sensitivity.

Eat Smaller Meals
Smaller, balanced meals help prevent sugar spikes and overeating.

A healthy breakfast reduces insulin resistance.

Achieve a Healthy Weight
Weight control can even help put diabetes into remission.

Exercise Regularly
At least 150 minutes of moderate exercise per week is recommended.

Quit Smoking
Smoking increases blood sugar and insulin requirements.

Get More Sleep
Aim for at least seven hours of quality sleep.

Reduce Stress
Stress hormones raise blood sugar. Yoga and meditation help.

Never abruptly stop insulin without medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making changes.
